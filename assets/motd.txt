========================================
   TFC4KIDS  -  Snowball Arena
========================================
 
  A safe, friendly first video game.
 
  No guns. No grenades. Just snowballs!
 
  This little server helps the youngest
  players learn what a video game is:
  move around, throw snowballs at friends,
  respawn, and try again. That's it!

  Open source: github.com -> tfc4kids
